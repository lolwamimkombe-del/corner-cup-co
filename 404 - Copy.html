Our story

A little corner of comfort on Main Street.

Corner Cup & Co is a small café built around the things that matter most — a proper cup of coffee, food made fresh that morning, and a room where you can slow down for an hour. We bake in-house, source locally where we can, and treat every visit like you're coming over for breakfast.
